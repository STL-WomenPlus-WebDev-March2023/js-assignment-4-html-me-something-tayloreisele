What's next?

My next career goal is to break into the tech industry and land a role as a web developer.

Technology is something I have always had a passion for. About 2 years ago I realized that I am no longer beging challenged in my roles in retail, so I wanted to make a change. I was extremely curious about the internet, how it works, and how websites are built. I started doing research in my spare time and shortly began learning HTML and CSS on my own, with the help of the internet. I was able to make static websites and make them look exactly how I wanted. Then, I was hooked! Fast forward a year, and I am exactly where I need to be to make the next jump in my career.
